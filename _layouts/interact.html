1906 Miller-Dervant Catalog Visualization

Imitative Identities

This visualization highlights the materials from the 1906 catalog of Spokane’s leading costume company.

The wall has two faces. The front organizes costumes by type; the reverse by national or ethnic costumes. Each card represents a category. Drag or select a card to answer. Responses flash red for incorrect answers and green for correct.

Select Costume Type or National or Ethnic Costumes at the top of the wall, or press F to flip between views. Sort categories or select Reveal Answer button to present the findings.

Source: Miller-Dervant Catalog, 1906, Miller-Dervant Company Records, 1896–1955, MsSC 198, Eastern Washington State Historical Society, Northwest Museum of Arts and Culture, Spokane, WA. Population data from the 1910 Abstract of the Thirteenth Census, Supplement for Washington State.

Controls
D / Drag When a card is selected, press D or drag the card to submit it as your answer
Long press On touch screens, hold a card to submit it as your answer (short tap opens card detail)
Tab Move keyboard focus between cards
Enter Open the focused card's detail view
Esc Close detail view or this introduction
F Flip the wall to the other face
