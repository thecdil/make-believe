Reading Interface Guide

Navigation

This essay uses a scrollytelling interface: as you read, the images and text citations respond to your position in the text.

  • Light and Dark Mode Select the light/dark toggle on the top right side of the reading interface depending on your reading preference.
  • ⊡
    Images and Item Pages Select any image to expand it. Select the "visit item" link in the image caption to visit the full record and learn more.
  • ↕
    Scroll Through Chapters Scroll past the end of a chapter to continue into the next or select which area you want to read in the sidebar table of contents.
  • ◫
    Interactive Visualizations Some sections contain interactive displays. Use the Skip → button in the lower-right corner to move past a visualization and continue reading.
  • ≡
    Contents Sidebar The sidebar marks two types of entries. Click any entry to jump directly to that content in the essay.
